Fair Districts PA - Senate Districts and Local Resolutions

To date, at least 13 state senators have co-sponsored SB 22, and more than 130 municipalities have adopted a resolution in support of an indpendent citizens' redistricting commission.

If your senator is a member of the State Government Committee, please contact them to ask for a hearing on SB 22. You can also see a similar map for the House, or learn how to pass a local resolution.
